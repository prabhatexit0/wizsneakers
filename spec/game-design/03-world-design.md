# World Design

## Region: Solecity Metropolitan Area

The game takes place in the **Solecity Metro** — a sprawling urban/suburban region where sneaker culture IS the culture. Every town has its own identity tied to a sneaker faction, and the connecting routes range from city streets to underground tunnels to open parks.

## Towns & Cities

### 1. Boxfresh Town (Starting Town)
- **Vibe**: Small, quiet suburb. "Where every journey starts fresh."
- **Key Locations**:
  - Player's House
  - Professor Sole's Lab (starter sneaker selection)
  - Mom's Sneaker Clinic (free healing)
  - Tutorial NPC houses
- **Sneaker Faction**: None (neutral)
- **Population**: ~20 NPCs
- **Size**: 30x20 tiles

### 2. Lace-Up Village (Boss 2)
- **Vibe**: Cozy retro neighborhood. Vintage shops and nostalgia.
- **Key Locations**:
  - Lace-Up Sneaker Clinic
  - Retro Rick's Vintage Shop (sells Retro-type sneakers and items)
  - The Lace-Up Gym — Boss: **DJ Throwback** (Retro specialist)
  - The Sneaker Museum (lore dump, teaches type matchups)
- **Sneaker Faction**: Retro
- **Population**: ~35 NPCs
- **Boss Level Range**: 12-15
- **Size**: 40x30 tiles

### 3. Kicksburg (Boss 3)
- **Vibe**: Gritty skatepark town. DIY energy, graffiti walls, half-pipes.
- **Key Locations**:
  - Kicksburg Sneaker Clinic
  - Thrasher's Board Shop (Skate items + sneakers)
  - The Kicksburg Ramp — Boss: **Ollie McFlip** (Skate specialist)
  - Secret skatepark area (hidden rare sneaker)
- **Sneaker Faction**: Skate
- **Boss Level Range**: 16-20
- **Size**: 45x35 tiles

### 4. Neon Springs (Boss 4)
- **Vibe**: Futuristic tech hub. Neon lights, clean lines, smart buildings.
- **Key Locations**:
  - Neon Springs Sneaker Clinic
  - ByteWear Boutique (Techwear items + sneakers)
  - The Neon Lab — Boss: **Dr. Firmware** (Techwear specialist)
  - Data Center dungeon (optional, rare Techwear sneaker)
- **Sneaker Faction**: Techwear
- **Boss Level Range**: 21-25
- **Size**: 45x35 tiles

### 5. Hypetown City Center (Boss 5 & 6)
- **Vibe**: The big city. Massive, bustling, diverse. All factions represented.
- **Key Locations**:
  - Hypetown Sneaker Clinic
  - The Mall (large shop with all faction gear)
  - Hypetown Convention Center — Boss: **Flex Queen** (High-Fashion specialist)
  - The Underground Market — Boss: **Shadow Broker** (mixed team, story villain encounter)
  - Sneaker Authentication Center (story-critical)
  - Player's apartment (unlocked mid-game, acts as second home base)
- **Sneaker Faction**: High-Fashion + Mixed
- **Boss Level Range**: 26-33
- **Size**: 60x50 tiles (largest map)

### 6. Grailheim (Boss 7)
- **Vibe**: Remote mountain town. Monks of sneaker purity. Ancient vibes.
- **Key Locations**:
  - Grailheim Sneaker Clinic
  - The Vault of Soles (legendary sneaker lore)
  - Temple of the Grail — Boss: **Grand Master Lace** (all types, balanced)
  - Hidden cave system (Genesis Grail clue)
- **Sneaker Faction**: Mixed/Legendary
- **Boss Level Range**: 34-38
- **Size**: 40x35 tiles

### 7. Resell Row (Boss 8)
- **Vibe**: Shady market district. Resellers, fakes, and hustle.
- **Key Locations**:
  - Resell Row Sneaker Clinic
  - The Black Market (rare items at inflated prices)
  - The Warehouse — Boss: **King Markup** (uses rare sneakers, tricky AI)
  - Counterfeit detection side quest
- **Sneaker Faction**: Mixed (opponents use rare/legendary sneakers)
- **Boss Level Range**: 39-44
- **Size**: 45x35 tiles

### 8. Pinnacle Tower (Endgame)
- **Vibe**: The ultimate challenge. A towering skyscraper.
- **Key Locations**:
  - Lobby (healing station, last shop)
  - Floors 1-4: The Elite Resellers (Elite Four equivalent)
  - Penthouse: The Champion — **The Collector**
- **Boss Level Range**: 45-55
- **Size**: 30x25 tiles per floor (5 floors)

## Routes

| Route | Connects | Length | Terrain | Wild Sneaker Levels | Features |
|-------|----------|--------|---------|-------------------|----------|
| Route 1 | Boxfresh → Lace-Up | Short (50x15) | Suburban sidewalks | 3-6 | Tutorial encounters, easy |
| Route 2 | Lace-Up → Kicksburg | Medium (60x20) | Suburban streets, small park | 8-14 | First tall grass zone, trainer battles |
| Route 3 | Fork → Kicksburg | Medium (55x20) | Skatepark, urban | 14-18 | Skate-type sneakers common |
| Route 4 | Fork → Neon Springs | Medium (55x20) | Tech corridor, labs | 14-18 | Techwear-type sneakers common |
| Route 5 | Neon Springs → Hypetown | Long (70x15) | Underground tunnels | 19-24 | Dark areas, rare encounters |
| Route 6 | Hypetown → Grailheim | Long (65x20) | Mountain path | 28-34 | Elevation changes, weather effects |
| Route 7 | Hypetown → Resell Row | Medium (55x20) | Market alleyways | 28-34 | Shady NPC encounters |
| Route 8 | Grailheim/Resell Row → Pinnacle | Short (40x15) | Skybridge | 38-44 | No wild encounters, dramatic |

## Tile Types

| Tile | Code | Walkable | Effect |
|------|------|----------|--------|
| Grass | `G` | Yes | None |
| Tall Grass | `T` | Yes | Random encounter chance (15% per step) |
| Path/Road | `P` | Yes | None, faster movement |
| Wall/Building | `W` | No | Collision |
| Water | `~` | No | Requires Surf item (post-game) |
| Ledge | `L` | One-way | Can jump down, not up |
| Door | `D` | Yes | Triggers map transition |
| NPC | `N` | No | Triggers dialogue on interact |
| Sign | `S` | No | Readable text on interact |
| Item Ball | `I` | Yes | Pickup, disappears after |
| Warp Tile | `@` | Yes | Teleport to paired tile |
| Encounter Zone | `E` | Yes | 100% scripted encounter (one-time) |
